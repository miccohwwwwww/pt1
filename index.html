8-Pearl Class '23 - '24







Overview


8-Pearl, often referred to as "Perlas ng Scilinian," is a grade 8 section of Honorato C. Perez Sr. Memorial Science High School. It is led by Ma'am Lilybeth Palon and Ma'am Maricris Valino. 8-Pearl is a section with a particular curriculum called the Special Program in Journalism (SPJ). 8-Pearl has its own 33 different pearls, each with unique abilities and talents other than writing. Some pearl can dance, sing, or play sports, while others can do all three. Writing is important in our section because it allows us to convey our thoughts and ideas to the rest of the world. We compete in a variety of journalism events, including press conferences. We basically thrive on writing.
